Matt Gilbert:
Making Moving Art
MFA Candidate featured

June 5, 2015

Art Studio graduate student Matt Gilbert is featured in an article and video on the UC Davis website. Read about his art and watch a video showing his kinetic sculptures in motion at UC Davis Today (June 3, 2015).

Matt Gilbert’s art, along with that of six other Master of Fine Arts candidates, can be seen in an exhibition June 5 – 21 at the Richard L. Nelson Gallery at UC Davis.
