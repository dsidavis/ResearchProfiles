Welcome to the Department of Dermatology

Comprehensive, thorough and personal care. 

Patients throughout the region count on the UC Davis Department of Dermatology to treat their common conditions such as acne, eczema and skin allergies, as well as complex and uncommon skin disorders. As a major referral center for Northern California and Nevada, we offer our patients a team of board-certified dermatologists with extensive experience and diverse and unique capabilities. Together, we are committed to meeting any and all of your dermatologic needs with expertise, with thoroughness, and above all, with compassion.
