The Department of Neurological Surgery at UC Davis Health System is home to internationally recognized experts in neurosurgical disorders. This includes a full-range of technologies to provide the most current and effective treatments for the entire spectrum of neurological disease. We provide expertise in neurosurgical care for the majority of the Northern California area. Both clinical and basic science research are foundational support for our focus on patient care.
