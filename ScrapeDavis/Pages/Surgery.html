The Department of Surgery encompasses a wide variety of surgical specialties. These include bariatric surgery, burn surgery,  cardiothoracic surgery,  gastrointestinal surgery,  pediatric surgery, plastic surgery,  transplant surgery,  trauma, acute care and surgical critical care surgery,  vascular surgery, and oncologic surgery. The department's extensive surgical expertise, in combination with ongoing teaching and research activities, provide ample resources and advanced surgical techniques for patient care.
