Department of Anthropology

— Categories: General Information

The UC Davis Anthropology Department is distinctive in our respect for multiple pathways through the discipline. We are organized into two Wings, a Sociocultural Wing including Linguistic Anthropology, and an Evolutionary Wing including Archaeology and Biological Anthropology.

Undergraduates: 

You can find out about Majoring in Anthropology, Minoring,  Courses we are currently offering, and Advising.

Graduate Students:

If you are interested in joining one of our programs, you can look at our Requirements, meet Current Graduate Students, and Apply for Admission online.

 

Faculty & Staff:

You can browse our Evolutionary Faculty, our Sociocultural Faculty, Staff, or search for specific persons (in the box in the upper left).
